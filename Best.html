HOTELS


Grand Hyatt

Located on the waterfront, this upscale hotel features stunning views and elegant rooms. Amenities include fitness center, and outdoor pool.

Rating:

  • The rooms were clean and comfortable. Would definitely stay here again.

Embassy Suites by Hilton

This all-suite hotel is conveniently located near the convention center and offers spacious rooms, complimentary breakfast, and a fitness center.

Rating:

  • Excellent hotel in a great location. The breakfast was delicious.
  • The rooms were clean and spacious. Would definitely recommend.

The Westin

Located on the bay, this modern hotel offers stylish rooms with water views, a rooftop pool, fitness center, and multiple dining options.

Rating:

  • Amazing hotel with great views. The rooftop pool was a highlight of our trip.

SPORT TEAMS


Tampa Bay Buccaneers (NFL)

The Buccaneers are a professional football team based in Tampa Bay. They have won multiple championships and are known for their passionate fans.

Rating:

  • Great team, always fun to watch!
  • The fans are the best!
  • Go Bucs!

Tampa Bay Lightning (NHL)

The Lightning are a professional hockey team based in Tampa Bay. They have won multiple championships and play in the state-of-the-art Amalie Arena.

Rating:

  • Love going to Lightning games, the atmosphere is electric!
  • Their Stanley Cup win was amazing!
  • Let's go Bolts!

Tampa Bay Rays (MLB)

The Rays are a professional baseball team based in St. Petersburg, just across the bay from Tampa. They have made several playoff appearances and play in Tropicana Field.

Rating:

  • The Rays always surprise me with their talent!
  • Tropicana Field is a fun place to watch a game.
  • Go Rays!

UNIVERSITIES


University of South Florida

USF is a public research university with multiple campuses in Tampa Bay area and known for the programs in business, engineering, and medicine.

Rating:

    I loved my experience at USF! The faculty and staff are incredibly supportive and the campus is beautiful

Hillsborough College

HCC is a community college with several campuses in the Tampa Bay area. It offers a wide range of programs and is a popular choice for local students.

Rating:

    HCC is a great option for those looking for affordable and flexible education. The professors are knowledgeable and the facilities are top-notch.

University of Tampa

UT is a private university located in the heart of downtown Tampa. It is known for its strong programs in business, communication, and the arts.

Rating:

    I had an amazing experience at UT. The professors are passionate about their subjects and the campus is stunning
